Henry G. Peterson

About

I am a graduate student working in Caltech's Environmental Science and Engineering option within the Division of Geological and Planetary Sciences. I received my B.A. in applied mathematics with an emphasis in fluid mechanics from UC Berkeley in 2019. At Caltech, I have worked with Jörn Callies to understand how enhanced turbulent mixing at the bottom of the ocean shapes the circulation of the abyss. I was recently awarded a fellowship from the Schmidt Academy for Software Engineering to continue development of an open-source finite element model of the abyssal ocean circulation. Check out the project highlight on their webpage!

Feel free to contact me at hgpeterson "at" caltech "dot" edu.
